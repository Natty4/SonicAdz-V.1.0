### Overview

This is a local payment-enabled ads platform for Telegram that empowers advertisers and channel owners with an automated, efficient system to buy, sell, and serve ads. The platform allows advertisers to target Telegram channels based on categories, language, budget, and CPM, while facilitating seamless local payment integration via mobile wallets and bank transfers. Unlike the original Telegram Ads, which only accepts TON coin, this platform offers a flexible, local payment solution and supports various ad formats for multiple types of Telegram content. The platform automates ad placement, channel matching, and performance tracking, ensuring transparency and fairness for all stakeholders.

### Features:

* **Local payment support** (via mobile wallets and bank transfers).
* **Automated ad placement and matching** based on targeting rules, budget, and CPM.
* **Fair ad review and approval** process.
* **Performance tracking** for both advertisers and channel owners.
* **Transparent escrow system** for ad payments and payouts.
